Brief overview of funding strategy

2. Red Hat Service-Based Model

Sell support, training, consulting, and managed hosting

Pros: Proven revenue, incentivizes quality, sustainable long-term

Cons: Requires sales team, may create tension between free/paid features

3. Foundation-Driven Model

Non-profit foundation (like Linux Foundation, Apache Foundation)

Funded by corporate sponsorships, grants, donations

Pros: Neutral governance, community trust, grant eligibility

Cons: Dependent on external funding, bureaucratic

4. Recommendation: Hybrid Model

Foundation for governance and IP management

Service-based for operational funding

Targeted grants from government/digital transformation funds
